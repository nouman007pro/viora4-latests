Basketball

The 2025 Women's NCAA Basketball Tournament began on March 19 and will conclude with the National Championship on April 6. The tournament starts with the First Four play-in round, featuring two No. 11 seeds and two No. 16 seeds. This year's top-seeded teams are UCLA, South Carolina, USC, and Texas. Columbia University faces Washington in a First Four matchup after narrowly losing last year. The Round of 64 begins on March 21. All games will be broadcast across ESPN and ABC networks, including ABC, ESPN, ESPN2, ESPNU, and ESPNews. ​
